Indigenous Australia

Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples have lived in Australia for over 65,000 years, making them one of the world's oldest continuous cultures.

European Colonization

European settlement began in 1788 with the First Fleet arriving at Botany Bay, establishing the colony of New South Wales.

Federation

On January 1, 1901, the six separate British colonies united to form the Commonwealth of Australia.

Modern Australia

Australia has evolved into a multicultural nation, playing significant roles in both World Wars and developing into a modern democracy.
